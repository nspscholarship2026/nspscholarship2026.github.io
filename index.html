🎯 Eligibility Criteria

To apply for NSP Scholarship 2026, students must meet certain eligibility conditions. These conditions vary depending on the scholarship scheme.

  • Must be an Indian citizen
  • Must be studying in a recognized institution
  • Family income should be within the specified limit
  • Must have valid documents

Students from SC, ST, OBC, Minority, and General categories can apply depending on the scheme eligibility.

📅 Important Dates

Students must be aware of important dates to avoid missing the application deadline.

  • Start Date: July 2026 (Expected)
  • Last Date: October 2026 (Expected)
  • Verification: November 2026
  • Payment Release: December 2026

📝 How to Apply for NSP Scholarship 2026

Applying for NSP Scholarship is simple and completely online. Follow the steps below:

  1. Visit the official portal
  2. Click on New Registration
  3. Fill personal details
  4. Upload documents
  5. Submit the application

Make sure to verify all details before submission to avoid rejection.

📂 Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Previous Marksheet

These documents are necessary for verification and approval of your scholarship application.

💰 Scholarship Amount

The scholarship amount depends on the scheme and level of education:

  • Pre Matric: ₹1,000 – ₹5,000
  • Post Matric: ₹5,000 – ₹20,000
  • Higher Education: ₹20,000+
